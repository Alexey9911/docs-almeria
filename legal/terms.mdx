Unipay is a privacy layer for Solana that enables users to transfer, swap, and distribute SOL and SPL tokens with graph-break privacy protection. The service consists of:

- **Web Client**: Non-custodial browser application
- **Routing Layer**: Stateless HTTP services for privacy-enhanced transactions

## 4. Privacy Model Disclosure

### Graph-Break Privacy

<Warning>
**Important**: Private flows break the direct on-chain link between source and destination addresses. However, they are not cryptographic mixers and do not protect against subpoena of the routing layer's logs.
</Warning>

### What We Protect Against

- Passive blockchain analysis
- Transaction graph correlation
- Address clustering techniques
- Balance surveillance

### What We Don't Protect Against
