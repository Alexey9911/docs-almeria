# Quick Start

Get up and running with Unipay in under 2 minutes. No account creation, no KYC, no deposits required.

## Prerequisites

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Solana Wallet" icon="wallet">
    Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or any Wallet Standard-compliant Solana wallet
  </Card>
  <Card title="SOL or Tokens" icon="coins">
    SOL, USDC, or USDT in your wallet for transactions
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Your First Private Transaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Visit [app.unipay.com](https://app.unipay.com) and connect your Solana wallet. We support all major wallets including hardware wallets via their respective adapters.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Flow">
    Select from three main features:
    - **Send**: Transfer tokens privately to another address
    - **Swap**: Exchange SOL for USDC/USDT anonymously  
    - **Payroll**: Batch payments to multiple recipients
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Privacy Mode">
    Choose between two execution modes:
    - **Wallet Mode**: Direct transaction, signed locally
    - **Private Mode**: Graph-break privacy via routing layer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute Transaction">
    Review the details and confirm. In Private mode, you'll receive an ephemeral deposit address and can track progress through our live status stepper.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example: Private Send

Let's walk through sending 1 USDC privately:

```bash
# 1. Connect wallet (browser UI)
# 2. Select "Send" 
# 3. Choose "Private Mode"
# 4. Enter recipient address: 7xKXtg2CW87d97TXJSDpbD5jBkheTqA83TZRuJosgAsU
# 5. Amount: 1 USDC
# 6. Confirm transaction
```

<Note>
Private mode transactions go through these stages: waiting → confirming → exchanging → sending → finished. Each stage is polled every 6 seconds and displayed in real-time.
</Note>
